About DeepInfer

DeepInfer is an open-source toolkit for deploying deep learning models that are designed specifically for medical image analysis and processing. It allows clinical researchers and biomedical engineers to deploy pre-trained deep learning models and use task-specific deep models, without the need for further software development and configuration. DeepInfer promotes reproducible resarch and dissimination of knowledge aquired with data-driven tools.

About Us

DeepInfer is managed by deep learning researchers at Surgical Planning Laboratory at the Harvard Medical School, Brigham & Women's Hospital and Robotics and Control Laboratory at University of British Columbia.
