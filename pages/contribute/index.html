Submitting Your Models

Just last year 3D Slicer has been downloaded more than 100,000 times*. Increase the visibility and exposure of your deep learning work and promote reproducible research by putting it in hands of hundred of thousand of medical researchers around the world. Package your deep learning models and contribute them to the DeepInfer model registry.

* 3D Slicer download statistics.



1. Dockerize your model.

The first step is to make a docker image containing your deep model, model weights, and required libraries and share it on DockerHub.

Dockerizing Guide ›


2. Create JSON model descritor.

DeepInfer supports a standard JSON descriptor fomrat which descibes model attributes, inputs, and outputs.

JSON Guide ›



3. Test your deployment.

Check your model deployment on DeepInfer supported operating systems and make sure it works flawlessly.

Deployment Testing Guide ›



4. Submit for review.

Add your JSON descriptor to the DeepInfer model registry by making a pull request. After reviewing your submission we will add your model to the registry.

Submission Guide ›
